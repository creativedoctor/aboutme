Medicine

Born in Braga in 1992, at 17 years-old I went on to attend Medical School at the University of Coimbra, Portugal, having graduated in 2016. In 2017 I moved to our capital, Lisbon, Portugal, where I’ll be doing my fellowship in neuroradiology for the next 5 years at Centro Hospitalar Lisboa Ocidental.

Research Science

Research appeared early in my life as I joined a research group at ICNAS in Coimbra, Portugal, while a medical student. I mainly participated in projects regarding brain imaging (anatomical and functional) for about 3 years, one of which led to what would be my master's thesis.

Data Science

Given our era of Big Data and its current and future impact in Health, I decided to learn data science in the beginning of 2017, as I believe it is (and will continue to be) extremely beneficial for decision-making in research and, consequently, in clinical practice. I mainly work with R (and R markdown) but I’m also learning Python.
